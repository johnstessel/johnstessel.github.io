About Me

I have been fixated on magic since the early age of eight. Seamlessly doing what appeared to be impossible moved quickly from a hobby to a passion. At the age of twelve, I began to channel my energy into the creative side of magic. By the time I was a teenager, I had thought up countless ideas for tricks and released 5 DVDs into the magic community, many of which became the best sellers. It's extremely humbling and I am so thankful.

I was fortunate enough to share my magic on SyFy's new reality show Wizard Wars, which first premiered in August of 2014. Wizard Wars is a show where magicians battle it out using nothing but ordinary objects and their creative ability to invent magic with these random items. This was an amazing experience and I am so happy that I was able to be a part of it.

All in all, my story is a never ending venture. Where one story ends, another begins. Where one door closes, another opens. Thank you for reading my story. I hope it helps influence yours.
